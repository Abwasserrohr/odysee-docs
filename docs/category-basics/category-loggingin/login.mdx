# Logging Into Odysee

Already have an account and are returning to Odysee? You'll click the **Log In** button on the top right corner.

![](https://i.imgur.com/BVk7SoC.jpg)

On mobile, you'll hit the person icon, which will show Log In in the dropdown menu.

![](https://i.imgur.com/vWURDEo.jpg)

You'll start by entering your email.

![](https://i.imgur.com/vpHKwo4.png)

On the password screen, you have a few options.

![](https://i.imgur.com/sxATuIE.png)

If you know your password and want to log in, you'll simply enter it and hit Continue. Alternatively, you can also have a [Magic link](https://help.odysee.tv/category-basics/category-loggingin/loginlinks/) sent to your email address or [reset your password](https://help.odysee.tv/category-basics/category-loggingin/passwordreset).
